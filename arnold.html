Seminar: The water capacity of Dyck paths

Speaker: Arnold Knopfmacher, University of the Witwatersrand, South Africa

Abstract: A Dyck path is a lattice path in the first quadrant with up steps (1,1) and down steps (1,-1) that starts and ends on the x-axis. We study the amount of water that would be retained in the valleys of the path if water was poured into the Dyck path shape from above.
