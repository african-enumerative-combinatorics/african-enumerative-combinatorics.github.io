About Us

African Enumerative Combinatorics Community (AECC) is a collaborative initiative to bring together mathematicians, both students and researchers, across Africa with a shared interest in counting problems, generating functions, and discrete structures.

Announcements

The African Enumerative Combinatorics Community (AECC) extends its heartfelt congratulations to our dear colleague and committee member, Yvonne Kariuki, on the successful defense of her PhD thesis!

This remarkable achievement is a testament to her dedication, resilience, and outstanding contribution to the field of combinatorics. We are incredibly proud to celebrate this important milestone with her and look forward to the impactful research, mentorship, and collaboration she will continue to offer as a scholar and role model in our growing community.

Well done, Dr. Kariuki! The future of African combinatorics is brighter because of you.

From The AECC Team
